1. Leap Year Checker

    Problem:

    Write a method that takes a year and checks whether it’s a leap year or not.

    Logic:

    A year is considered a leap year if:

    • It is divisible by 4,
    • But not divisible by 100,
    • Unless it is also divisible by 400

    Examples:

    Input Output Reason
    2000 Leap Year Divisible by 400
    1900 Not Leap Year Divisible by 100 but not 400
    2024 Leap Year Divisible by 4 and not divisible by 100
    2023 Not a Leap Year Not divisible by 4

  2. Character Type Checker

    Problem:

    Accept a character input and check if it is:

    • An Uppercase Letter
    • Abig
    • A Lowercase Letter
    • A Digit
    • A Special Character

    Logic:

    • 'A'-'Z' --> Uppercase

    • 'a'-'z' --> Lowercase

    • '0'-'9' --> Digit

    • Anything else --> Special Character

    Examples:

    InputOutput
    AUppercase Letter
    aLowercase Letter
    1Digit
    @Special Character
